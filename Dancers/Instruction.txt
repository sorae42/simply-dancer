You must prepare an animation sheet of such character you want to add and name their file 
corresponding to the directory name, with the frame width, height and "(doubleres)" if 
your character can be added with double the resolution.

For example, if your character has a sheet of 14 x 2 and named "NMReimu", you must 
name them and put them in the folder like this: `NMReimu 14x2 (doubleres).png`.

Then drag the file of your character to this folder and they should be in the player options menu!

TIP:
- Your dancer should only have 2 "animation cycles" (as in they only swing back and forth).
- Make sure your texture is exported in even numbered resolution (e.g. 1280x128)

Some example has been provided for you to work with.
